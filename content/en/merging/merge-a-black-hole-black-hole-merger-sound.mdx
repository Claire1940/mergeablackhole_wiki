<Section icon={Map} title="Introduction to Merge a Black Hole">

Merge a Black Hole is a simulation game on Roblox that lets players merge a black hole by absorbing objects. The game's core mechanic is to expand your black hole and unlock new abilities, all while collecting resources and progressing through the game.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Understanding Black Hole Upgrades">

### Black Hole Size Upgrade
Increasing the size of your black hole is crucial for progress. This upgrade allows you to absorb larger objects, which in turn increases your collection speed and resource gain.

| Upgrade | Description | Impact |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Small Black Hole | Absorbs small objects | Low resource gain, slow growth |
| Medium Black Hole | Absorbs medium objects | Moderate resource gain, moderate growth |
| Large Black Hole | Absorbs large objects | High resource gain, fast growth |

### Collection Speed Upgrade
This upgrade enhances your black hole's ability to collect objects, increasing your resource gain and allowing you to progress faster.

| Upgrade | Description | Impact |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Slow Collection | Standard collection speed | Average resource gain, average growth |
| Fast Collection | Increased collection speed | High resource gain, fast growth |

### Resource Upgrade
By upgrading your resource collection, you can increase the value of the objects you absorb, leading to faster growth and more resources.

| Upgrade | Description | Impact |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Basic Resource | Standard resource value | Average resource gain, average growth |
| Enhanced Resource | Increased resource value | High resource gain, fast growth |

### Progression Upgrade
This upgrade prioritizes certain upgrades, helping you unlock stronger growth opportunities and reach higher levels faster.

| Upgrade | Description | Impact |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| Standard Progression | Standard progression speed | Average growth |
| Fast Progression | Increased progression speed | Fast growth |

</Section>
