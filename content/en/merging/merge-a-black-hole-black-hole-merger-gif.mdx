## Rebirth

### Rebirth Guide

Rebirth is a major progression system in Merge a Black Hole that allows players to restart their current growth cycle in exchange for permanent progression advantages. Use rebirth strategically to increase future farming speed and reach higher black hole levels faster.

### Steps for Rebirth

1. **Grow Your Black Hole**: Merge objects, consume items, and collect enough resources to increase your black hole power before attempting a rebirth.
2. **Reach Rebirth Requirements**: Complete the required progression goals needed to unlock the rebirth option and prepare your reset.
3. **Activate Rebirth**: Reset your current progress to receive permanent bonuses that improve future growth speed.
4. **Repeat With Better Efficiency**: Use rebirth bonuses to collect resources faster and unlock higher progression milestones.
