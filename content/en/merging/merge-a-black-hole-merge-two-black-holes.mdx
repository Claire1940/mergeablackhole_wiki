### Merging Black Holes
Merging black holes is the core mechanic of the game. To merge two black holes, simply combine them using the merge button. The result will be a new black hole with combined properties.

It's important to note that merging black holes requires resources, such as dark energy. The more powerful the black hole, the more resources it will consume. Additionally, the shield around the black hole will decrease with each merge, so be sure to keep it fully charged to avoid being attacked.

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Guide to Merging Black Holes

### Step 1: Collect Micro Holes
Start by collecting micro holes, as they are the easiest to merge and combine with other holes. Once you have a few micro holes, you can start merging them to create dark wisps.

### Step 2: Merge Dark Wisps
Once you have a few dark wisps, merge them to create gravity pits. Gravity pits are significantly stronger than dark wisps and will allow you to progress further in the game.

### Step 3: Merge Gravity Pits
Continue merging gravity pits to create void pebbles. Void pebbles are rare and powerful, and will provide you with additional options for merging and combining black holes.

### Step 4: Merge Void Pebbles
Merge void pebbles to create gravity wells. Gravity wells are very powerful and will allow you to deal massive damage to your enemies.

### Step 5: Merge Gravity Wells
Merge gravity wells to create dark stars. Dark stars are extremely powerful and will allow you to create the ultimate black hole, the Infinity Well.

### Step 6: Create the Infinity Well
Combine dark stars with other black holes to create the Infinity Well. The Infinity Well is the most powerful black hole in the game and will allow you to destroy everything in its path.
