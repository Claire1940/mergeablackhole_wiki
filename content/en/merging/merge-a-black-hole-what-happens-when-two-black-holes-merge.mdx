### 🎯 Merge a Black Hole Basics

Merge a Black Hole is a simulation game where players merge black holes to expand them and unlock new capabilities. The goal is to grow your black hole as large as possible by absorbing objects and merging them into the black hole.

#### 🌌 Core Gameplay

- **Absorb Objects**: Move your black hole around the map and absorb available objects to increase its size and gain resources.
- **Merge Black Holes**: Once your black hole has absorbed enough objects, you can merge it with another black hole to increase its size further.
- **Upgrade Your Black Hole**: Use the resources you collect to upgrade your black hole, unlocking new abilities and increasing its growth potential.

### 🚀 Efficient Merging and Upgrading

To succeed in Merge a Black Hole, you need to merge black holes efficiently and upgrade your black hole effectively. Here are some tips to help you achieve this:

#### 🌟 Upgrade Priorities

- **Collection Speed Upgrade**: This upgrade increases the speed at which your black hole collects objects, allowing you to merge more frequently.
- **Resource Upgrade**: This upgrade improves the value of the resources you collect, maximizing your progress.
- **Progression Upgrade**: Prioritize upgrades that unlock new growth opportunities and improve your overall progression.

#### 🌟 Rebirth System

The rebirth system allows you to reset your current progress and receive permanent bonuses that improve your future growth speed. Use rebirth strategically to increase your farming speed and reach higher black hole levels faster.

### 🌟 Essential Gameplay Tips

- **Prioritize Growth Speed**: Focus on upgrades and actions that increase how quickly your black hole can consume objects and generate resources.
- **Merge Efficiently**: Combine available objects strategically to unlock stronger items and improve your overall progression rate.
- **Use Resources Wisely**: Save currency for valuable upgrades instead of spending resources on low-impact improvements.
- **Optimize Farming Routes**: Choose efficient areas and object collections to maximize the amount of resources gained over time.
- **Plan Rebirth Timing**: Avoid resetting too early and wait until the rebirth reward provides meaningful progression benefits.
