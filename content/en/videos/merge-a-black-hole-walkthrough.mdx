### Gameplay Mechanics
Merge a Black Hole is a physics-based game where you merge black holes to clear obstacles and progress through levels. The objective is to collect sparkles, which can be merged to create larger black holes. These larger black holes then help you clear paths and destroy barriers.

### Essential Gameplay Steps

| Step Number | Step Description |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Start by collecting sparkles scattered around the level. |
| 2 | Merge the collected sparkles to create a small black hole. |
| 3 | Use the small black hole to merge with other black holes, gradually increasing in size. |
| 4 | Clear obstacles and collect additional sparkles to continue merging and progressing. |
| 5 | Be mindful of your shield power, as it will protect you from incoming obstacles. |

### Strategy Tips

#### Prioritize Merging Small Holes
Start by merging the smallest black holes to increase your shield power and make it easier to handle larger obstacles. This will give you a solid foundation to build upon as you progress.

#### Manage Your Shield Power Wisely
Your shield power is crucial for survival. Always be mindful of your shield's status and prioritize merging black holes that will help you regain shield power or provide health upgrades.

#### Keep an Eye on the Score
The score is determined by the number of obstacles you destroy and the efficiency of your black hole merging. Aim to maintain a high score to unlock additional in-game rewards.

#### Use Quests to Your Advantage
The game features various quests that can provide health upgrades, shield power, and other benefits. Complete these quests to enhance your gameplay experience.
