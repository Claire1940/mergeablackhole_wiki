## Introduction
Welcome to the ultimate guide to Merge a Black Hole, a captivating Roblox simulation game where players merge a black hole to absorb objects and grow its size. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about expanding your black hole, maximizing progression, and unlocking new abilities.

### Basic Gameplay
Merge a Black Hole revolves around the concept of expanding a black hole by absorbing objects scattered across the map. As you collect objects, your black hole grows, allowing you to absorb larger objects and increase your resource collection. The ultimate goal is to become the largest black hole in the game, outpacing your competitors and unlocking unique rewards.

## Expand Your Black Hole
### Black Hole Growth
To expand your black hole, you must first absorb objects of increasing size. Start by moving your black hole around the map and collecting small objects to increase your size. As your black hole grows, you'll be able to absorb larger objects, such as asteroids and planets, which contribute more resources and increase your growth rate.

### Efficient Farming Routes
To maximize your growth speed, it's crucial to optimize your farming routes. Look for areas with a high concentration of objects and plan your movements to ensure you collect as many resources as possible in a short amount of time. Remember to prioritize objects that provide the most resources to speed up your growth.

## Progression and Upgrades
### Upgrade Guide
Upgrades play a vital role in your progression. They enhance your black hole's size, collection speed, and overall efficiency. Here are some essential upgrades to consider:

- **Black Hole Size Upgrade**: Increase the size of your black hole to absorb larger objects and unlock new areas of the map.
- **Collection Speed Upgrade**: Improve your collection efficiency and reduce the time needed to gather resources.
- **Resource Upgrade**: Increase the value gained from collected objects and improve overall progression.

### Prioritizing Upgrades
When choosing upgrades, prioritize those that will provide the most significant impact on your growth speed. Focus on upgrades that enhance both your black hole's size and collection speed to ensure efficient resource gathering.

## Rebirth System
The rebirth system allows you to restart your current growth cycle in exchange for permanent progression advantages. Use rebirth strategically to increase future farming speed and reach higher black hole levels faster.

### Rebirth Process
- Grow your black hole to increase its power before attempting a rebirth.
- Complete the required progression goals to unlock the rebirth option and prepare for your reset.
- Activate rebirth to reset your current progress and receive permanent bonuses that improve future growth speed.
