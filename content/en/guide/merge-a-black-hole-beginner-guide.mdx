### 🌌 Overview of Merge a Black Hole

Merge a Black Hole is a captivating simulation game on Roblox that challenges players to merge objects to expand a black hole and unlock new abilities. In this beginner's guide, we will cover the basics of merging, resource collection, and progression strategies to help you grow your black hole and achieve higher levels of success.

### 🌍 The Basics of Merging

**Step 1: Start Collecting Objects**
To begin your journey in Merge a Black Hole, move your black hole around the map and absorb available objects. These objects can range from small asteroids to larger planets, each providing different amounts of resources.

**Step 2: Grow Your Black Hole**
Use the resources collected from merging objects to expand your black hole. As your black hole grows, it will be able to absorb larger objects, leading to increased resource gains and faster progression.

**Step 3: Improve Progression Speed**
Focus on upgrading your black hole's abilities to improve collection speed and resource value. This will allow you to merge more objects and gain resources more quickly, leading to faster progression.

### 🚀 Efficient Farming Routes

To maximize your resource collection, it's important to choose efficient farming routes. Here are some tips to help you get started:

- **Identify High-Yield Areas**: Look for areas on the map where you can collect a large number of objects in a short amount of time.
- **Prioritize Resource Value**: Focus on merging objects that provide the highest value, such as rare minerals or energy crystals.
- **Plan Your Route**: Plan your farming route to minimize backtracking and maximize the number of objects you can merge.

### 🌟 Upgrade Prioritization

Upgrades play a crucial role in your progression in Merge a Black Hole. Here are some upgrades to consider:

- **Black Hole Size Upgrade**: Increase the size of your black hole to absorb larger objects and gain more resources.
- **Collection Speed Upgrade**: Improve the efficiency of your black hole's collection process.
- **Resource Upgrade**: Increase the value of resources gained from merging objects.

### 🤔 Frequently Asked Questions

**Question 1: How do I increase my black hole's size?**
Answer: To increase your black hole's size, merge objects and use the collected resources to expand it. As you progress, you can unlock upgrades that further increase the size of your black hole.

**Question 2: What is the best farming route for beginners?**
Answer: For beginners, it's best to start in areas with small objects and gradually move to areas with larger objects. Focus on merging objects that provide the highest value to maximize your resource collection.
