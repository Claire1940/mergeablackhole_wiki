## 🎮 Introduction to Merge a Black Hole
Merge a Black Hole is a game that revolves around merging black holes to create stronger and more powerful ones. The objective is to merge black holes to increase your spawn cap and level tier, allowing you to create more powerful black holes and ultimately dominate the game.

## 🌌 Understanding the Gameplay
### 🌟 Merging Black Holes
The core gameplay mechanic in Merge a Black Hole is merging black holes. To merge two black holes, you need to bring them close together and wait for them to combine. The result is a stronger black hole with increased damage potential.

### 🌟 Spawn Cap and Level Tier
Your spawn cap determines how many black holes you can have at once, while your level tier determines the maximum level of black holes you can create. Increasing your spawn cap and level tier is crucial for progressing in the game.

### 🌟 Black Hole Types
There are several types of black holes in the game, each with its own unique abilities and strengths. Some of the most powerful black holes include the Gravity Pit, Void Pebble, Gravity Well, and Void Core.

## 🛠️ Step-by-Step Guide to Merging Black Holes
### 🌟 Initial Setup
1. **Start with Small Black Holes**: Begin by merging small black holes to increase your spawn cap and level tier.
2. **Keep an Eye on Your Shield**: Ensure your shield is active to protect yourself from attacks.

### 🌟 Core Progression
1. **Merge Dark Wisps**: Merge Dark Wisps to create Gravity Pits, which are powerful black holes with a Saturn ring.
2. **Combine Gravity Pits**: Merge Gravity Pits to create Void Pebbles, which are even more powerful.

### 🌟 Advanced Tactics
1. **Create Gravity Wells**: Merge Void Pebbles to create Gravity Wells, which are extremely powerful black holes.
2. **Combine Gravity Wells**: Merge Gravity Wells to create Void Cores, which are the most powerful black holes in the game.
