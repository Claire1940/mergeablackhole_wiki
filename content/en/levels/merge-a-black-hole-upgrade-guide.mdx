## 🎯 Understanding the Upgrade System

### Types of Upgrades
Merge a Black Hole offers a variety of upgrades to enhance your gear. Here's a breakdown of the most common types:

| Upgrade Type | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| Shield       | Increases your protection against damage |
| Health       | Boosts your maximum health |
| Damage       | Increases the damage you deal to enemies |
| Speed        | Increases your movement speed |

### Prioritizing Upgrades
When it comes to upgrading your gear, it's important to prioritize the right upgrades based on your playstyle and the challenges you face. Here's a general guideline:

- **If you're facing a lot of damage**: Prioritize shield and health upgrades.
- **If you want to deal more damage**: Focus on damage and speed upgrades.
- **If you want to move quickly**: Prioritize speed upgrades.
